About us

LOVI is an audiobook and audio content service specializing in spiritual culture, wisdom traditions, and esoteric knowledge. Its curated catalog includes nonfiction and fiction, classics of the world's wisdom traditions, works by contemporary authors, podcasts, lectures, audio dramas, and other in-depth audio content.

Oral tradition has played a central role throughout human history. People have gathered around fires to listen to stories, myths, and teachings that helped them understand the world and their place within it. Although our environment has changed, the need for meaningful narratives has not disappeared.

LOVI is a modern home for this ancient tradition. We do not aim to compete with large mainstream audiobook services; instead, we focus on subjects that are often overlooked by them: philosophy, history, mythology, spiritual traditions, folklore, art, and culture.

The service is intended for anyone who wishes to explore the world, culture, and themselves on a deeper level. LOVI seeks to build bridges between ideologies, religions, and worldviews while highlighting traditions and perspectives that unite humanity.

Our first published content includes literature, lecture series, podcasts, interviews, articles, poetry, and other original productions.

LOVI's content is produced professionally in collaboration with authors, researchers, teachers, and other cultural creators. Our goal is to build a high-quality audio library that preserves, renews, and transmits spiritual and cultural heritage to future generations.

We also believe in a fairer creative economy. That is why LOVI pays its creators competitive compensation and is developing a model in which content creators can also participate in the long-term development of the service.

Thank you for joining us on this journey.
